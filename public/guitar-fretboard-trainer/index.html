Questions

Guitar fretboard trainer FAQ

Is GuitarRun free?

Yes. You can open the trainer and begin practicing in your browser without paying or installing software.

Do I need an audio interface?

No. An audio interface gives a clean signal, but you can also use your device microphone near an acoustic guitar or amplifier.

Is my guitar audio uploaded?

No. Pitch detection runs on your device, and your audio is not uploaded.

What can I practice?

You can practice major, minor, pentatonic, multi-position, timing, root-note, memory, and fretboard-navigation exercises.
